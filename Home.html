Jatin Mor Jewels feature an array of exquisitely designed jewellery that is both classic and delicate, exuberant and elegant. The range of jewellery at Jatin Mor is regal and unique to compliment your versatility and also seamlessly blends into your wardrobe.

Our Legacy

The history of jewellery making at Jatin Mor dates back to the eighteenth century. Risen in the land of beauty and affluence, the Zenab of Nizams, Jatin Mor jewellers inculcates in itself, the rich history and cultural influence of Hyderabad.

Our Values

Capturing the visual essence of Indian culture in its intricate designs, Jatin Mor jewels are carefully crafted giving life to the stones and the metal. Jewellery at Jatin Mor is an embodiment of finesse and exceptional craftsmanship.

The Craftsmen

Craftsmanship at Jatin Mor is exquisite and elegant. Witness the finest quality of time-defying jewellery made from a harmonious amalgamation of modern and antique designs. Every piece of jewellery at Jatin Mor is an ode to skilful craftsmanship and ethereal designs. Through the years, Jatin Mor has been involved in creating alluring pieces of jewellery. The expertise, skill and the spirit of artisanal excellence are seen from initial inspirational sketch to the final designs.
